Programmer, Musician

Roger Walton Harris III was born in Baton Rouge, Louisiana on December 9th, 1989. He only spent about two years in Louisiana before moving to San Antonio, Texas, where he would spend a majority of his childhood. He enjoyed being outside, playing sports, riding his bike, or exploring with his friends. He also enjoyed playing board games, checkers and chess with his dad, playing video games, or just messing around on the computer.

In 2004 he moved to Daphne, Alabama, where he attended Daphne high school. He graduated in 2008, and decided to attend Auburn University. Not knowing exactly what he wanted to do with his life, he switched majors from business to journalism to psychology and then to graphic design, which he stayed in for a year. But even graphic design wasn't really for him, so he pursued a degree in Computer Science. Never having programmed
a single line of code before, he instantly took to it and loved it.

Auburn taught him the basics of programming, but he wanted more. In January of 2014, Roger moved to New York City with his girlfriend, Allison, and their two cats, Pixel and Java. After driving a 14-foot U-haul truck 15 hours with two sedated cats in the front cab in order to beat a blizzard that was about to hit New York City, Roger settled in and started his internship at Health Guru Media. Here, he learned the ins and outs of Web Development. After his internship (and a merger with Kitara Media), Roger was brought on as a full time junior developer to further develop his skills.

In his free time, Roger enjoys spending time with his girlfriend, watching movies, reading a good book, and listening to music. He also enjoys playing music. He has been playing the bass guitar since the Spring of 2005, and has since then picked up guitar and some drums as well. While attending college at Auburn University, he
was in 4 different bands, each very uniquely different from the others.

The first band he joined, Blame Sydney!, was a ska/punk rock band whose main purpose was to play fun music for their friends and have a good time doing it. For a brief period in time, he also joined the band SummerTrees with his friend Richard from Blame Sydney. He played lead guitar for the few months they were together. After they went their seperate ways he focused mainly on Blame Sydney. The second band he was in, Gypsies With Knives, he joined because they were in dire need of a new bassist. He had known the band members after previously playing with them in Blame Sydney, so it was an easy fit for him. Gypsies With Knives consisted of an eclectic mix of classic rock, punk rock, reggae, funk, and blues. Finally, in his last semester of college, he helped out a friend by playing for his sister's cover band. Entitled the Cheri Blossoms, they were a cover band that played classic rock, country, alternative rock and pop songs.
